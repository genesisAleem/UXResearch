About Me

Designing at the edge of digital & experience

I'm Olalekan, a UI/UX designer at Duo, a digital and experiential marketing agency in Lagos. I work across the intersection of product design, 3D visualisation and brand experience.

My clients span some of the biggest names on the continent — Unilever, Red Bull, BetKing, Bet9ja, Maltina, Johnnie Walker and more. I believe every touchpoint should earn the user's attention.
